## What they are

Biostimulators are a category of treatments designed to stimulate the skin's
own collagen — gradually, over time, rather than in a single visit. Amy offers
two: **Radiesse**, an injectable made of calcium hydroxylapatite, and **PDO
threads**, dissolvable threads placed beneath the skin.

They share one idea. Instead of an immediate change, a biostimulator works with
your body's own biology, prompting collagen that builds slowly. It's a longer
view — which is exactly why this line begins with a conversation rather than a
booking.

## A longer view of structure

Biostimulators appeal to people thinking in terms of gradual change — an
approach measured in months and planned across visits, rather than one done all
at once. Whether it fits you, and which treatment makes sense if so, is decided
with Amy — your history and your goals in front of her.

## Two ways in

The two treatments reach the same idea differently. **Radiesse** is delivered by
injection; **PDO threads** are placed beneath the skin and dissolve gradually
after. They suit different faces and different goals — Amy explains what each
involves, plainly, and recommends one, both, or neither based on what's right
for you.
