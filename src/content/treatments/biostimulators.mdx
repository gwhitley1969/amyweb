## What they are

Biostimulators are a category of treatments designed to stimulate the
skin's own collagen production gradually, over time. Amy offers PDO
Threads — dissolvable threads placed beneath the skin — and Radiesse,
an injectable biostimulator.

## Who they're generally for

Adults thinking in terms of gradual change rather than immediate volume
often ask about this category. The two approaches differ meaningfully,
so this line begins with a conversation: whether a biostimulator fits —
and which one — is decided with Amy in a consultation.

## Individualized, with Amy

Nothing in this category is off the shelf. Amy Palacios, FNP, plans
every treatment individually, under clinician supervision, at her
Harrisburg studio serving the greater Charlotte area.
