## What it is

Body contouring here means Evolve — a non-invasive, device-based
treatment. Sessions use applicators placed on the skin, with no needles
involved; the device is designed for skin tightening and muscle toning.
What a session involves, and how a course is structured, is explained
plainly before anything is scheduled.

## Who it's generally for

Adults considering a non-surgical approach to a specific area often ask
about this category. Whether Evolve fits your goals — and what a course
would involve for you — is decided with Amy in a consultation.

## Individualized, with Amy

Amy Palacios, FNP, plans and performs every treatment herself, under
clinician supervision, at her Harrisburg studio — minutes from
Charlotte.
