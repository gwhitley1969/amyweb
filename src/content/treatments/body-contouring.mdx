## What it is

Body contouring here means one thing: **Evolve**, a non-invasive treatment
delivered by a device rather than a needle. Amy lists it on her menu exactly as
it works — *tighten and tone*.

Nothing is injected and nothing is cut. Hands-free applicators are placed on the
area and held in position while you lie still; the device works through the
surface of the skin.

## Two kinds of energy

Evolve applies two different energies, which is why the service is named for two
things rather than one:

- **Radiofrequency** — the applicators deliver uniform heating to the skin and
  the layer beneath it.
- **Electrical muscle stimulation (EMS)** — pulses prompt involuntary
  contractions in the muscle group under the applicator.

One is aimed at the skin. The other is aimed at the muscle underneath it. Which
of the two matters more for you — or whether Evolve is the right route at all —
is a conversation with Amy, not a decision made from a price list.

## What a session is like

You lie down, and the applicators are strapped into position over the area. From
there it's hands-free: nothing is being held against you, and Amy sets the level
with you before it starts.

The radiofrequency reads as warmth. The muscle stimulation reads as tightening —
the muscle contracts and releases on its own. A session runs roughly 30 to 60
minutes, depending on the area.

## Where it's used

The applicators are sized for the larger areas of the body — the abdomen,
flanks, thighs, arms, and buttocks. Which area is right for you, and whether
this treatment fits your goals at all, is settled in a consultation before
anything is scheduled.

## Two ways to book it

Evolve is priced two ways: a single session, or a course of six. Amy walks
through both structures honestly — including what she would not recommend — and
neither one is the default.

## Individualized, with Amy

Amy Palacios, FNP, plans and performs every treatment herself, under clinician
supervision, at her Harrisburg studio in the greater Charlotte area. It starts
with a consultation — free, as always.
