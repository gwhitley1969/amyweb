## What they are

Dermal fillers are injectable gels placed beneath the skin to add volume
or refine contour in a specific area. Common treatment areas include the
lips, cheeks, jawline, chin, and the under-eye area.

## Who they're generally for

Adults considering added volume or a change of contour in a particular
feature. Filler work is anatomy-first: what suits your face — and what
to leave alone — is decided with Amy in a consultation.

## Individualized, with Amy

Every plan is individual. Amy Palacios, FNP, plans and performs each
treatment herself, under clinician supervision, at her Harrisburg studio
in the greater Charlotte area.
