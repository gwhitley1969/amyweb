## What they are

Dermal fillers are injectable hyaluronic-acid gels placed beneath the
skin to add volume or refine contour in a specific area. Hyaluronic
acid occurs naturally in skin, where it helps hold hydration — as a
filler, it lends structure and subtle volume. Common treatment areas
include the lips, cheeks, jawline, chin, and the under-eye area.

## After weight loss

Weight loss changes faces, too. Losing volume — including on a GLP-1
journey — can leave lines and folds more visible than they were. If
that's part of your story, bring it to a consultation: filler is one of
the tools Amy weighs when deciding what, if anything, to place. Amy
also offers medically supervised
[weight-loss programs](/services/weight-loss-glp-1).
