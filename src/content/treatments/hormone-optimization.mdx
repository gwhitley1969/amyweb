## What it is

Hormone optimization here means bioidentical hormone replacement therapy
— BHRT — delivered through the Biote method, which uses pellets placed
under the skin. Amy is a Biote-certified provider. Care starts with a
hormone lab draw, so every decision rests on your actual levels, not a
guess.

## Who it's generally for

Many people first ask about hormones when something feels persistently
off — fatigue, poor sleep, brain fog, or mood swings. Noticing a pattern
is a reason for a conversation, not a conclusion: whether hormone
therapy makes sense for you is decided with Amy in consultation.

## Individualized, with Amy

Hormone care is as individual as medicine gets. Amy Palacios, FNP, plans
and supervises every course personally at her Harrisburg studio, in the
greater Charlotte area.
