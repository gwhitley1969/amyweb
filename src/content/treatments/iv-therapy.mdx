## What it is

IV therapy delivers vitamins and other nutrients by infusion, with
vitamin shots as the quicker option. The menu includes Myers' Cocktail,
the Immunity IV, vitamin shots, Glutathione, B12, and NAD IV. What's in
each is explained plainly before anything is given.

## Who it's generally for

People book IV therapy for all kinds of personal reasons. What each drip
contains — and what to expect from a visit — is something Amy walks
through before anything begins. Unsure where to start? A consultation is
free on request.

## Individualized, with Amy

Amy Palacios, FNP, administers every infusion herself at her Harrisburg
studio, minutes from Charlotte.
