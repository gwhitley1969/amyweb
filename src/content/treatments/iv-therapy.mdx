## What it is

Vitamins and minerals, given two ways. An **infusion** goes into a vein and
runs in over the course of your visit. A **shot** is a single injection, and
it's over in moments.

Amy's menu is short and named plainly. What's in each one is gone through with
you before anything is given — and where a blend has several ingredients, they
are listed on the card above rather than left vague.

## Two ways it's given

An infusion runs through a small catheter placed in a vein in the arm. You stay
seated while it goes in, and Amy places the line herself.

A shot is an injection into muscle — no catheter, no sitting and waiting.

Which form something comes in isn't a preference. It follows from what is being
given.
