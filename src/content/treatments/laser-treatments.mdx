## What they are

"Laser treatments" is the name this whole family of light- and energy-based
skin work usually goes by, and it's the name Amy uses on her menu. The device
behind it is precise about what it actually sends into the skin: **Venus
Versa**, a platform whose applicators work with intense pulsed light and
radiofrequency energy — close relatives of the laser, each shaped to a
different job. One platform, three distinct applications: skin resurfacing,
photo-rejuvenation, and fine-line treatment.

## Skin resurfacing

The NanoFractional RF applicator works through the skin's surface: tiny pins
channel radiofrequency energy to the deeper layers, where collagen is made.
It's used where the concern is the appearance of wrinkles, enlarged pores,
uneven texture, or scarring — including acne scarring — and the applicator is
designed for all skin types and tones. Whether it's the right tool for *your*
skin is a consultation conversation.

## Photo-rejuvenation

Different job, different physics. The intense pulsed light applicators —
FDA-cleared devices — deliver brief pulses of light aimed at pigment and
visible vessels near the surface: the appearance of sun damage, brown spots,
small capillaries, redness and discoloration. The skin is cooled in real time
while the light is delivered; comfort is designed into the applicator, not
added afterward.

## Fine lines

The third application pairs two energies in one FDA-cleared applicator:
multi-polar radiofrequency, which warms the skin, and pulsed electromagnetic
fields — a pairing the manufacturer calls (MP)². Nothing enters the skin; the
applicator is passed across the surface, and the treatment is designed to
soften the appearance of facial fine lines and wrinkles.

## Three tools, one conversation

Which applicator fits — resurfacing, light, or the fine-line pairing; one of
them, a sequence, or none — is a consultation decision, not a menu pick. Amy
also offers [PiXel8-RF microneedling](/services/skin-rejuvenation) on her
skin-rejuvenation line; where the two overlap, the consultation decides which
tool suits your skin — or whether the honest answer is neither.

## Individualized, with Amy

Amy Palacios, FNP, plans and performs every treatment herself, under
clinician supervision, at her Harrisburg studio in the greater Charlotte
area. It starts with a consultation — free, as always.
