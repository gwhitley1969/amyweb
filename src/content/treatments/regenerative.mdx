## What they are

"Regenerative" is the umbrella term for a group of distinct treatments.
PRP and PRF are prepared from a small sample of your own blood. PDRN is
derived from salmon DNA. Illuma, VAMP, and Rejuran round out the line.
What each one is — and is not — is something Amy explains plainly before
anything is scheduled.

## Who they're generally for

People usually come to this category curious about skin texture and
tone. More than any other line, this one begins with a conversation:
what a given treatment involves, and whether it makes sense for you, is
decided with Amy in a consultation.
