## What they are

PRP — platelet-rich plasma — is prepared from a small sample of your
own blood. Amy offers it two ways: PRP on its own, and PRP combined
with microneedling. What each visit involves is explained plainly
before anything is scheduled.

## Who they're generally for

People usually come to this category curious about skin texture and
tone. This line begins with a conversation: what a given treatment
involves, and whether it makes sense for you, is decided with Amy in a
consultation.
