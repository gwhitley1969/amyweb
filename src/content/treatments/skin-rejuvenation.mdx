## What they are

Two clinician-performed approaches to skin texture and tone. Pixel8 is
a microneedling device that pairs fine needles with radiofrequency
energy, delivered in the studio. Medical-grade chemical peels are
clinician-applied solutions that exfoliate the outer layer of the skin.
What each involves — and what a visit looks like — is explained plainly
before anything is scheduled.

## Who they're generally for

Adults curious about skin texture and tone often ask about this
category. The two approaches differ meaningfully, and skin itself
differs more: which one fits yours — if either — is decided with Amy in
a consultation.

## Individualized, with Amy

Amy Palacios, FNP, plans and performs every treatment herself, under
clinician supervision, at her Harrisburg studio in the greater
Charlotte area.
