## What it is

This is a medically supervised weight-management program built around
prescription medications. GLP-1 therapy — Semaglutide and Tirzepatide —
anchors the program, and Phentermine is also offered where it fits a
plan. Retatrutide is on the menu as well; it is investigational and
not FDA-approved, as the disclosure above states. Every one of these
is a prescription medication, and every one belongs inside a
supervised plan — never on its own.

## Who it's generally for

Adults considering medical support for weight management. Prescription
care is never one-size-fits-all: whether any of these medications
belongs in your plan — and which one — is a clinical decision Amy makes
with you in consultation, with your history in front of her.

## Individualized, with Amy

This line is consultation-routed by design. Amy Palacios, FNP, plans and
supervises every program individually at her Harrisburg studio — minutes
from Charlotte.
