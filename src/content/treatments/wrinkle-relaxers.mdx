## What they are

Wrinkle relaxers are prescription neuromodulators — injectable treatments
used to temporarily soften dynamic lines, the creases that form with
repeated muscle movement. Common treatment areas include the forehead,
frown lines, and crow's feet.

## Who they're generally for

Adults who want expression lines softened while keeping natural movement
often consider this category. Whether it fits you — and which product
suits your anatomy and goals — is a clinical decision, made with Amy in
a consultation.

## Individualized, with Amy

There is no standard plan here. Amy Palacios, FNP, assesses your goals
and plans every treatment individually, under clinician supervision, at
her Harrisburg studio — minutes from Charlotte.
